Direct Transport Standard Testing Services

Welcome to the Direct Transport Standard Testing Services page. This page provides a list of registered health IT developers who have agreed to offer their Direct Transport Standard testing services to providers and implementers. ONC will evaluate this approach to determine whether it could be expanded to include additional interoperability testing in the future.

Health IT Developers interested in registering their ONC Certified Health IT systems, click on Developer Registration tab above.

Questions or concerns regarding:

  • SITE Direct Transport Standard Testing Services should be submitted to ONC.
  • Developers interested in providing testing services should be submitted to xvendor.exchange@hhs.gov
  • ONC Health IT Certification Program should be submitted to ONC.Certification@hhs.gov

Implementers can Click here for Instructions on how to use the Direct Transport Standard Testing Services for interoperability testing.
